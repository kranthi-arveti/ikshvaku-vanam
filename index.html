About the Farmhouse

Two levels, one grove — built around stone, water and open sky

Ikshvaku Vanam sits in the heart of Future City, Hyderabad — just 20 minutes from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, with none of the long outskirts drive that usually comes with a countryside escape. Land this open, this close to the city, is rare.

The farmhouse is built across two levels. Above ground, a low bungalow of natural stone and glass holds a bedroom and a lavish living area made for parties and long evenings with friends. Below ground, set into a sunken stone courtyard with a cluster of boulders and a still reflecting pool, sits a fancy kitchen and dining space — an atmospheric setting for meals unlike anything else nearby. A rooftop pool with a jacuzzi and wide lawns round out the grounds.

The centerpiece of it all is the stone amphitheater built into the courtyard steps — the main highlight of the property, and the perfect stage for live performances, music and entertainment under the open sky.

It's a place for weekend getaways, family get-togethers, and small celebrations — with the comforts of a proper home and none of the distance.
